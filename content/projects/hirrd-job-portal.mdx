## Product Context

Hirrd was built as a role-based hiring workflow where recruiters and candidates operate with different goals but share a consistent product language.

## Engineering Focus

- Role-first navigation and route protection
- Clean application flow from job discovery to submission
- Form and listing UX designed for practical use

## Build Decisions

I prioritized clarity in routing and data boundaries so role-specific actions stay predictable. That decision improved both user experience and backend contract readability.

## What I Would Improve Next

- Add pipeline analytics for recruiter funnel drop-off
- Introduce better candidate profile ranking signals
- Expand workflow notifications for status changes
